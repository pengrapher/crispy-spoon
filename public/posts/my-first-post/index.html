DACs are everywhere

October 20, 2024

DACs

With the immense ownership of portable audio devices, a DAC chip is quite likely embedded in them. Your mobile or cell phone - it has a DAC. Your bluetooth earbuds, it’s got one too.

For every headphone, mobile game device, phone, audio transmitter, TV, streamer, smart speaker, amplifier, tablet or computer in 2024 that you own a Digital Audio Converter chip is embedded in it to convert audio bits and bytes to the the sound we hear. No longer are DACs just in computers and laptops with a standalone chip. It grew exponently along with the pervasive use of mobile devices and the shift towards wireless audio, with bluetooth standards.
